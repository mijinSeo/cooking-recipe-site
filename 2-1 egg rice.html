Today's menu is egg rice with soy sauce.

Food ingredients
1 egg, 2T cooking oil, 180g rice, 10ml soy sauce, 10ml sesame oil

  1. Pour oil into a hot frying pan and fry eggs.
  2. Place 1 on the prepared rice.
  3. Sprinkle soy sauce and sesame oil.
  4. Finished!
